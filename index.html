Research Interest

My primary research interests lie in the design and manufacturing of wearable electronics for human body monitoring, Human-computer Interaction (HCI), and Human-robot Interaction (HRI). Within these research directions, I have mainly worked on three research areas.

01

High performance wearable sensors based on functional structures/materials

02

Manufacturing technologies to fabricate 3D electronics

03

Development of HCI and HRI applications
